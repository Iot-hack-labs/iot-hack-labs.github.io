### Victure PC420 Baby Monitor

# Requirements

- Victure PC420 baby monitor camera with power cord
- USB to TTL adapter (Shikra, DSD Tech USB to TTL, etc.)
- Micro SD (at least 20 MB)
- Micro SD to USB adapter
- 3 Female to male jumper wires
- Computer
